About Marhaba AI

Marhaba AI is an AI application that helps you understand the people you love and improve your relationship with them.

We tried to make the app as simple and as intuitive as possible to give you the best user experience.

After you sign in and answer the onboarding questions you'll arrive at the home screen which is your hidden laboratory for understanding people around you.

In the home screen you can create chats about people you want to understand. Wonder why that friend doesn't want to accept help from anyone, why that classmate tries their best to avoid talking to you or why you used to have a good relationship with your best friend and now they seem to not like you as much.

You can explore these questions in Marhaba AI's chats.

Understanding MBTI

MBTI (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator) is a personality framework that helps you understand how people think, make decisions and interact with the world.

It describes personality through four preferences: how people gain energy, process information, make decisions, and structure their lives.

After answering a set of questions each person is mapped to one of sixteen personality types. These types don't measure intelligence or ability but patterns of perception, motivation, and behavior.

MBTI helps explain why people communicate differently react differently to stress and value different things in relationships.

By understanding personality differences MBTI can improve self-awareness, empathy, communication, and relationship dynamics without labeling anyone as right or wrong.

Understanding Yourself

Marhaba AI is not only a tool for understanding other people it can also help you understand yourself.

  • Why do you like that artist so much?
  • Why can't you forget that person no matter how hard you try?
  • Why do you feel like no-one understands you?

These are questions you can ask in the personal chat.

You have the option to delete the chat afterwards if you don't want your information to be saved on the app.

We have systems that keep your information private but we also want you to be in control of what information gets saved in our cloud functions.

You can read more about how data is collected and stored on Marhaba AI in settings => privacy policy.

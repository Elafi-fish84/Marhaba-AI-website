Privacy Policy

At Marhaba AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name and profile picture you choose
  • MBTI Information: Your personality type determined through quizzes
  • Conversations: Messages you exchange with AI in the application
  • Preference Settings: Response length, response style, language, theme (light/dark)
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system to help improve the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our app services
  • Save your conversations and preferences
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Enhance AI performance
  • Communicate with you about the app (when necessary)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • We use Firebase (Google service) to securely store your data
  • Conversations and profile files are stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage
  • All data is encrypted in transit
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data

4. Purchases and Subscriptions (Google Play)

Marhaba AI uses Google Play Billing to manage paid subscriptions

  • The subscription may be offered after the \"Let's start your journey with Marhaba AI\" screen, and AI chat features are activated after you subscribe
  • Payments and card details are processed by Google Play. We do NOT store your full payment card information on our servers
  • We only receive limited information from Google Play, such as subscription status (active/expired), plan type, and basic transaction metadata needed to enable or disable features inside the app
  • If you do not have an active subscription, the ability to send new messages to the AI (chat box) will be disabled, although you may still be able to view past conversations or use other parts of the app
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription via your Google Play account settings, and payments are subject to Google Play's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For storage and authentication
  • OpenAI: For processing conversations using GPT-3.5-turbo
  • Google Play Billing: For managing in-app subscriptions and payments

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data saved in the app
  • Request deletion of your account and data (you can delete chats manually or contact us)
  • Export your data (future feature)
  • Opt out of certain information collection (note that this may affect app functionality)

7. Data Deletion

  • You can delete individual conversations directly from the app
  • To delete your entire account and data, please contact us at: marhabaai@gmail.com
  • We will delete your data within 30 days of your request

8. Children's Privacy

  • The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
  • We will notify you of any material changes through the app
  • Your continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: marhabaai@gmail.com

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.
